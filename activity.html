BULLYING

Cause of bullying

  • A desire to contol or attetion
  • Insecurity or problems at home
  • Lack of undrstanding or empathy

Effect of bullying

  • Poor academic performance
  • Low self-esteem and anxiety
  • Thoughts self harm
  • Depression

Solution

  • Speak up - Tell a teacher, counselor, or parent.
  • Support each other - Stand up for classmates who are being bullied.
  • Create awareness - Schools should run anti-bullying programs.
  • Promote kindness - Respect and empathy can stop bullying before starts.
